About Aaron

Aaron is a multi-disciplinary designer with a focus on product design. With over 15 years in the field, he’s had the opportunity to work at agencies, consultancies and in-house but currently prefers helping out start-ups. Some previous experiences include IDEO, Google, R/GA, Frog, Verizon, Citi, GE, Betaworks and Instapaper.

About This Site

This site is an evolving work-in-progress with selected studies and samples from past projects. Equal parts process and final hand-off, it’s meant to showcase thought patterns that are unique for each project in order to create a variety of work.
